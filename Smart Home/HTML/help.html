Guidelines for the products


Never use metallic pencils or rulers,or wear rings or metal watchbands when working
with electrical equipment.

Equipment producing a "tingle" should be disconnected and reported promptly for
repair.

Drain capacitors before working near them amd keep the short circuit on the terminals
during the work to prevent shock.
